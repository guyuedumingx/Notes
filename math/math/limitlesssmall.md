$$1 -cosx \to \frac{1}{2}x^2 $$

$$(1+\alpha x)^{\frac{m}{n}} \to 1+\alpha \frac{m}{n}x $$

##双曲正弦
$$sh~x = \frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{2}$$  
##双曲余弦  
$$ch~x = \frac{e^x+e^{-x}}{2}$$
##双曲正切
$$th~x = \frac{sh~x}{ch~x} = \frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{e^x+e^{-x}}$$

$$ch^2x - sh^2x = 1$$
